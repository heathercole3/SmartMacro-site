SmartMacro Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SmartMacro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how SmartMacro collects, uses, and protects information when you use the SmartMacro mobile application (the "App").

By using SmartMacro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

SmartMacro may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information – When you use the app, we may collect email addresses (if you create an account) and account login information.
  • Nutrition and Health Data – SmartMacro allows users to log nutrition-related information such as food entries, calories and macronutrients, recipe information, weight logs, nutrition goals, and preferences.
  • Device Information – We may collect limited technical information including device type, operating system, app version, and diagnostic data.
  • Usage Data – Information about how the app is used, including interactions with features and tools, may be collected to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

SmartMacro uses collected information to:

  • Provide nutrition tracking features
  • Calculate calorie and macro targets
  • Generate nutrition insights and reports
  • Improve the functionality and performance of the app
  • Manage subscriptions and purchases
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

AI and Automated Processing

SmartMacro may use artificial intelligence and automated tools to estimate nutritional values from user inputs such as food descriptions, recipes, or URLs. These estimates are generated automatically and may not always be accurate.

The information provided by the app is intended for informational purposes only.

Subscriptions and Payments

SmartMacro offers subscription-based access to certain features. All payments are processed through Apple's in-app purchase system via Apple. SmartMacro does not store or process payment information directly.

Third-Party Services

SmartMacro may use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

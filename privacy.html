Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartMacro ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the SmartMacro mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Food and nutrition data entered by users
  • Basic account information if an account is created
  • App usage analytics to improve performance
  • Device information required to operate the application

How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Provide macro and nutrition tracking features
  • Improve functionality and performance of the app
  • Generate nutrition insights and estimates
  • Provide customer support

AI and Automated Processing

SmartMacro may use artificial intelligence services to estimate nutritional values from user inputs such as text descriptions or recipe links. These estimates are generated automatically.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party providers for services including:

  • Analytics
  • AI processing
  • Subscription and payment management

Subscriptions

The app may offer paid subscriptions that unlock additional features. Payments are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data, but no system can guarantee complete security.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data by contacting support@smartmacro.app.

Children's Privacy

The SmartMacro app is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.
